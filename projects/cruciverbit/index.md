### Description

This is the official webpage of the Cruciverb-IT - Crossword Solving Shared Task organized at EVALITA 2023. Cruciverb-IT is the first shared task proposed at EVALITA 2026 on crossword puzzle solving. We propose two tasks: i) answering clues extracted from Italian crosswords; ii) autonomously solving Italian crossword grids.

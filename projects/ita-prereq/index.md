### Description

The ITA-PREREQ dataset is the first Italian dataset of concepts extracted from Wikipedia and annotated with prerequisite relations. Specifically, ITA-PREREQ is a conversion of the English AL-CPL dataset and contains a total of 455 concepts and 1,495 prerequisite relations extracted from four Wikipedia macro-domains: Data Mining, Geometry, Physics, and Numerical Analysis.
